THE DARK KNIGHT

A Legendary Battle Between Chaos and Justice

The Dark Knight is a 2008 superhero film directed by Christopher Nolan and is the second installment in his Batman trilogy. The story follows Bruce Wayne as Batman, who faces his greatest challenge yet when a criminal mastermind known as the Joker emerges in Gotham City. Unlike any ordinary villain, the Joker thrives on chaos and aims to break the city’s spirit by turning its heroes into villains. With intense action, moral dilemmas, and a powerful performance by Heath Ledger as the Joker, The Dark Knight is widely regarded as one of the greatest superhero films of all time. It explores themes of justice, fear, and sacrifice in a dark and realistic tone.
